This is a Development Website

Crack your D&D 4e characters with Bandit 4e. Create and manage your characters easily by manually registering only a few details about each. Bandit 4e is not a replacement for your character sheet, but is designed to help you quickly track all of the details associated with playing D&D or other role playing games – without erasing a hole in your character sheet. It will not roll for you – that's half the fun of playing RPGs – and Bandit wants to keep it that way.
